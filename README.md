# Europa: A Collaborative Platform for Music Producers

## Description
Much like GitHub for software development, Europa allows music creators to upload, manage, and remix their tracks with ease. Producers can fork tracks, collaborate with others, and maintain a clear history of changes—all while ensuring their original work is protected and under their control. With a focus on community, creativity, and accessibility, Europa empowers music producers to share their work, collaborate seamlessly, and engage with a supportive network of fellow artists. The platform will also incorporate features similar to Bandcamp, providing users with customizable public pages for streaming their music and selling merchandise.

## Key Features

- **Track Upload and Management**: Easily upload music files, manage different versions, and maintain a clear history of changes.
- **Collaboration and Forking**: Collaborators can "fork" tracks or stems for remixing, allowing original producers to approve or deny modifications.
- **Public Profiles**: Each producer can create a custom public page to showcase their music, stream tracks, and eventually sell merchandise.
- **Version Control**: Keep track of changes to your music projects, similar to how developers manage code in GitHub.
- **Cross-Platform Availability**: A web application built with Next.js and a desktop application using Rust for enhanced performance and accessibility.
- **Future Integration of Decentralized Storage**: Explore options for decentralized file storage to give producers more control over their work and ensure permanence.

## Development Plans

1. **MVP Development**: Focus on building the core features of track upload, version control, and collaboration using AWS S3 for storage.
2. **Rust for Performance**: Utilize Rust for backend logic to handle version control and permissions efficiently.
3. **User Feedback**: Launch the MVP to gather feedback from early users and iteratively improve the platform based on their input.
4. **Integration of Decentralized Storage**: Research and implement decentralized storage solutions like IPFS or Arweave to provide long-term archival options for tracks and stems.
5. **Public Profiles and Merch**: Develop customizable public pages for producers to showcase their music and offer merchandise, expanding the platform's capabilities.
6. **Community Building**: Foster a vibrant community of music producers, encouraging collaboration, sharing, and feedback to enhance creativity and growth.
